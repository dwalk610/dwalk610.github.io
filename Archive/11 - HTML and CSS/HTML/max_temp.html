Max Temp

Overall, city temperatures are higher as you approach the equator (0 deg Latitude). The current season in the southern hemisphere is Spring which means that it is currently being exposed to more direct sunlight than the northern hemisphere. This explains why northern hemisphere cities temperatures drop at a faster rate the farther they get from the equator, than cities in the southern hemisphere
